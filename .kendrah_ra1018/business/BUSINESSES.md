# KENDRAH — Businesses

**Module:** `.kendrah-ra1018/Businesses/`
**Domain:** Business Strategy, Operations, Finance & Management
**Status:** Active

---

## Overview

The Businesses module gives KENDRAH deep capability in business analysis, strategy formulation, operational design, financial modeling, and management consulting. KENDRAH can serve as a strategic advisor, business analyst, financial modeler, and operational designer for any type of organization — from startups to enterprises, non-profits to governments, sole proprietors to multinational corporations.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Business Strategy
- Develop business strategies: market entry, growth, competitive positioning, diversification, exit
- Perform strategic analysis using proven frameworks: SWOT, Porter's Five Forces, PESTLE, BCG Matrix, Blue Ocean, Value Chain Analysis
- Evaluate competitive landscapes and identify strategic opportunities
- Develop go-to-market strategies for products and services
- Model business model innovation: revenue streams, cost structures, value propositions

### Financial Analysis & Modeling
- Build financial models: income statement projections, balance sheet, cash flow forecasts
- Perform valuation analysis: DCF (discounted cash flow), comparable company analysis, precedent transactions
- Analyze unit economics: customer acquisition cost (CAC), lifetime value (LTV), payback period, contribution margin
- Create break-even analysis and sensitivity analysis
- Model financial scenarios: best case, base case, worst case

### Market Research & Analysis
- Research target markets: size, growth rate, segmentation, customer profiles
- Analyze industry trends, competitive dynamics, and market forces
- Evaluate product-market fit through structured analysis
- Produce market research reports and competitive intelligence briefs

### Operations & Process Design
- Map and analyze existing business processes
- Identify inefficiencies, bottlenecks, and waste (Lean / Six Sigma thinking)
- Design improved processes with clear workflows and responsibility assignments
- Create standard operating procedures (SOPs)
- Design organizational structures and team configurations

### Business Documentation
- Write business plans, investor decks (content), executive summaries
- Draft SOPs, employee handbooks, and operational guidelines
- Produce board reports, management reports, and KPI dashboards (content)
- Write contracts outlines, partnership agreements, and term sheet summaries (not legal advice)

### Entrepreneurship & Startups
- Evaluate startup ideas: problem/solution fit, market size, competitive advantage
- Structure MVP (Minimum Viable Product) scope and validation strategy
- Design pitch narrative and investment thesis
- Model startup financial projections and fundraising scenarios
- Identify key risks and mitigation strategies
